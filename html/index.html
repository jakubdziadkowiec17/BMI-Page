About BMI

BMI is the body mass index. It is calculated using the user's body weight in kilograms, divided by the square of the height in meters. This is a common way to check and evaluate weight problems in adults. The given coefficient then allows you to compare the values in a table showing the appropriate weight ranges and categories. It allows you to determine whether a given person is underweight, overweight or normal according to the given parameters. This is one of the useful and simple ways to control your health.

Advantages

Simple and easy measurement

BMI allows you to easily check your body mass index. It is easy to calculate. It can be calculated in seconds.

Quick analysis of results

BMI enables quick and simple result analysis. The results are easy to understand and the table clearly presents the criteria.

Obesity treatment monitoring

BMI allows you to regularly monitor your obesity treatment. The results can help assess changes in body weight.

Recommendations

BMI is a body mass index that should not be compared in some cases. It is not used in children. This happens because of rapid changes in growth. It is not used by pregnant women. It is also not comparable in older people. It should also be avoided by people with a high level of muscle tissue. BMI does not distinguish fat mass from muscle mass, which has a significant impact on the final result. Therefore, it is not an accurate measurement of body weight.
